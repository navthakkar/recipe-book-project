roasted pumpkin seeds

serving size: no serving size is ever enough for me haha

recipe origin: family!

tags: vegan snack south american <3

ingredients

  • 1 medium pumpkin (or just the seeds from the pumpkin!)
  • salt
  • extra virgin olive oil

instructions

  1. cut pumpkin & scrape out seeds (use the pumpkin for soup - recipe here!)
  2. place innards & seeds on a colander & run under water to rinse & separate the seeds from everything else
  3. dry overnight on paper towel with some salt sprinkled over them
  4. measure pumpkin seeds in a cup measure & place in a medium saucepan.
  5. add 2 cups of water & 1 tbsp of salt for every half cup of pumpkin seeds (add more if you want seeds to be saltier!
  6. bring the salted water & pumpkin seeds to a boil. then, let simmer for 10 mins
  7. remove from heat & drain
  8. preheat oven to 400ËšF
  9. coat a roasting pan with 1 tsp of olive oil
  10. spread seeds over the pan in a single later & toss to coat with oil
  11. bake on the top rack until seeds begin to brown (5-20 mins depending on the size of the seeds)
  12. remove & cool before serving
