banana peel bacon

serving size: n/a

recipe origin: tiktok it doesn't taste like chicken

tags: vegan snack ¯\(ツ)/¯

ingredients

  • 2 very ripe banana peels
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce (low sodium if less salty)
  • 1 tbsp honey or maple syrup
  • ½ tsp smoked paprika (or chili powder)
  • ½ tsp garlic powder
  • 1tbsp light oil
  • piri piri powder to taste

instructions

  1. !!! use bananas that are very ripe (the riper the banana, the better the flavor – pick yellow bananas with lots of brown spots)
  2. to prepare the banana peels, peel the banana & tear into about 4 stops per banana. use a spoon to lightly scrape off the white inside part of the banana peel leaving just the peel
  3. make the marinade by mixing the soy sauce, maple syrup, smoked paprika, & garlic powder together in a dish that will fit the peels
  4. add the banana peels & toss to coat. let marinade for a minimum of 10 mins, but as long as a few hours
  5. when ready to cook the bacon, heat the oil in a large skillet or frying pan over medium heat. When hot, add the peels & fry a couple of minutes per side, until they are golden & bubble up a little (it may get a little smoky as the sugars will burn so make sure to turn on your hood fan)
  6. remove from pan & dry on paper town. they will get crispier as they cool
  7. serve & enjoy!

navya's thoughts

even though this doesn't exactly taste like bacon, it is a pretty cool recipe to experiment with. additionally, it makes sure you use all parts of the banana & reduces waster, which is always cool!
