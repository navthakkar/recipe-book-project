dutch baby pancakes

serving size: serves 1

recipe origin: crowded kitchen’s instagram reel

tags: breakfast dessert <3

ingredients

  • ½ cup all purpose flour
  • ½ cup milk (room temp - can use non-dairy)
  • 2 eggs (room temp)
  • 2 tbsp granulated sugar
  • ½ tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp lemon zest (optional)
  • 2 tbsp butter (can use non-dairy)
  • 2 tbsp butter (can use non-dairy)

for roasted strawberries:

  • 1 pint strawberries, quartered (~2 cups)
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp lemon zest (can be replaced with some lemon/lime juice to taste!)

instructions

  1. preheat oven to 425°f
  2. place a 9” oven-safe skillet in the oven while it preheats (i personally think cast iron is best!)
  3. meanwhile, add all the pancake ingredients except butter to a blender & blend until smooth. let sit for 10-20 minutes while the oven heats
  4. toss together the strawberries, lemon zest (or lemon/lime juice!) & sugar, then transfer to a sheet pan
  5. once oven is heated, add butter to the pan & swirl until it’s all melted, then pour in the pancake batter right away
  6. place the pancake & strawberries in the oven. Backe for 15-20 mins, until the edges are puffy & golden brown & the strawberries are jammy
  7. top the strawberries & serve warm with maple syrup, honey, or powdered sugar! enjoy!
