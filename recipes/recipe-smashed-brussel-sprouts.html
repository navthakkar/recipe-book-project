smashed brussel sprouts

serving size: serves 2

recipe origin: halle burns' tiktok

tags: vegan snack appetizer

ingredients

  • a bag of brussel sprouts
  • ¼ cup of olive oil
  • 1 tbsp sriracha (or szechuan sauce!)
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • salt & pepper to taste
  • onion powder
  • garlic powder
  • dipping sauce: 1 part plant-based yogurt (or hummus), 1 part lime juice (& some piri piri powder if you have some!)

instructions

  1. preheat oven to 450˚F
  2. steam the brussel sprouts for 20-25 mins
  3. mix the olive oil, sriracha, soy sauce, salt, pepper, onion powder & garlic powder in a bowl. mix in the brussel sprouts & coat them fully
  4. add to the lined pan & mash the sprouts with a glass. make sure the smashed sprouts are coated in sauce
  5. roast for 20-25 mins at 450˚F in oven
  6. mix dipping sauce ingredients & serve!

yummy crispy snack that is super easy to make!
