vodka sauce pasta

serving size: serves 2

recipe origin: tiktok foods by jane

tags: italian pasta entrée

ingredients

  • ½ box (½ lb) pasta
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp garlic powder or 2 minced garlic cloves
  • 2 tbsp plain vodka (replace with broth if unavailable)
  • 3 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 cup creamy oat milk
  • 2 tbsp cream cheese
  • ½ cup freshly grated parmesan cheese + some for serving
  • ¼ cup reserved pasta water

instructions

  1. bring a large pot of heavily salted water to a boil & cook the pasta according to package instructions. once cooked, take ¼ cup of pasta water out, stir in some spinach & let cook. then strain it & put some olive oil in to avoid sticking
  2. in a large skillet on medium heat, melt the butter & add the tomato paste & garlic. cook until the paste is brick red in color
  3. add the vodka & whisk (the boozy taste & flavor of the vodka will evaporate here)
  4. after about 3-5 mins of evaporating & cooking, add half of the oat milk & whisk. turn the heat to simmer while you bring it to a bubble
  5. add the rest of the oat milk & whisk again (you can add more milk if you want it saucier)
  6. once mixed, add the cream cheese & grated parmesan cheese. mix until melted
  7. if the sauce is super thick, add in some of the salty/starchy pasta water to loosen things up & create an extra silky texture
  8. add the pasta to the sauce, combine & serve hot, sprinkled with some freshly grated parmesan cheese

navya's thoughts

i like to think of vodka alla pasta (or vodka sauce pasta!) as a middle ground between tomato pasta & alfredo pasta! one the the most delicious pasta recipes ever.
