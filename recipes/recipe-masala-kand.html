masala kand (purple yam)

serving size: serves 2

recipe origin: home! cookpad

tags: vegan indian snack street food

ingredients

  • 250 g purple yam
  • oil for coating
  • lime juice

for kand masala:

  • ½ tsp black salt
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp black pepper powder
  • ½ tsp red chili powder
  • ¼ tsp hing
  • 1 tsp toasted cumin powder
  • ½ tsp dry mango powder
  • ½ tsp garam masala

instructions

  1. clean & cut yam to preferred size
  2. cook them: either fry them, or steam them & oven cook them (alt: air fry them)
  3. mix all the spices mentioned to make kand masala
  4. serve with kand masala, lime juice & cooked kand!

notes

  • if, wherever you are, fresh kand is not available, most indian stores sell a pre-cut, frozen packet of kand which you can use!
  • the best way to eat this (i have found!) is having the masala & lime juice as dips, & coat each bite to your heart's desire!
