butternut squash soup

serving size: serves 1

recipe origin: hebbars kitchen

tags: french soup fall comfort food

ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil (or neutral flavored oil or butter)
  • 1 onion (thinly sliced)
  • 2 cloves garlic (full)
  • 2 cup (400 g) butternut squash, sliced (or any pumpkin of your choice)
  • paprika powder (to taste)
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp pepper (crushed)
  • 2 cups vegetable broth (can also use water)
  • cream to garnish (can also use greek yogurt, cream cheese, etc.)
  • chopped cilantro for garnish

instructions

  1. in a large pot, heat 2 tbsp olive oil
  2. further, sauté 1 thinly sliced onion until they shrink
  3. sauté 2 cloves of garlic
  4. add a dollop of the cream of your choice
  5. add 2 cups (400 g) of sliced butternut squash
  6. add salt, pepper, & paprika
  7. sauté for a minute or two, until it slightly changes color
  8. add 2 cups of water & mix well
  9. cover & boil for 15 mins on medium heat
  10. cook the squash well & allow to cool lightly
  11. transfer to the blender & blend to smooth paste
  12. pour the soup into a bowl & garnish with a tbsp of cream
  13. serve soup sprinkled with black pepper & chopped cilantro

notes

  • this soup can be made with any kind of fall pumpkin or squash. butternut squash is only the most common, along with the yellow squash pumpkin!
  • also goes really well with some garlicky toast or grilled cheese!

navya's thoughts

i've personally found a yummy, hot, spicy bowl of butternut squash soup goes best on cold, rainy fall days!
