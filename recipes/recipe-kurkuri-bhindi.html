kurkuri bhindi (crispy indian okra)

serving size: serves 2

recipe origin: family! love laugh mirch

tags: indian entrée <3

ingredients

  • 500 g Indian bhindi (cut lengthwise or in equal circles)
  • 2 ½ tbsp oil
  • ½ tsp turmeric powder
  • ½ tsp amchur powder (or chat masala)
  • ¼ red chili powder
  • 2 tsp dhanajeeru powder
  • salt to taste

instructions

  1. pre-heat oven to 425˚F
  2. lightly oil a large baking sheet/pan & set aside
  3. cut the bhindi & arrange it in a single layer on the baking sheet
  4. bake for 15 mins on the middle rack. remove, flip the okra & bake for 5-7 mins more. they should be crisp & dark green
  5. if cooking on a stove top, heat the oil, then layer the bhindi on the pan. do not stir. let the bhindi sit until the bottom is cooked, then flip them over
  6. remove from heat, add all the masala in, with adding salt last
  7. serve with roti & yogurt! (trust me, it's delicious)

notes

  • bhindi can also be made on the stove top by lightly pan frying it first, then mixing with masalas. however, baking it makes sure all the bhindi is cooked uniformly.
  • pro tip: mix the bhindi with dahi (indian yogurt) or greek yoghurt & eat it with some fresh & warm rotis! you will not regret it
