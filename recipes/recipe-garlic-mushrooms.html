garlic mushrooms

serving size: serves 2

recipe origin: café delites

tags: american appetizer

ingredients

  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter
  • ½ tbsp olive oil
  • ¼ of an onion, chopped
  • ½ pound (250 g) cremini or button mushrooms
  • 1 tbsp dry white wine (optional)
  • ½ tsp fresh thyme leaves chopped
  • 1 tbsp chopped parsley
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • salt & pepper to taste

instructions

  1. heat the butter & oil in a large pan or skillet over medium-high heat
  2. sauté the onion until softened (about 3 mins)
  3. add the mushrooms & cook for about 4-5 ins until golden & crispy on the edges
  4. pour in the wine & cook for 2 mins, to reduce slightly
  5. stir in thyme, 1 tbsp of parsley & garlic. cook for a further 30 secs, until fragrant
  6. season generously with salt & pepper to taste
  7. sprinkle with remaining parsley & serve warm
