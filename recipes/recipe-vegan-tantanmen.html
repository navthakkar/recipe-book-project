vegan tantanmen

serving size: serves 2

recipe origin: new york times cooking

tags: vegan japanese ramen entrée

ingredients

  • kosher salt & black pepper to taste
  • 6 ounces dried ramen noodles
  • 2-3 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • ½ package extra-firm tofu, frained & sliced crosswise, ¼ inch thick
  • 4 large shiitake mushrooms, trimmed & thinly sliced
  • 2 cups vegetable stock
  • 1 (2-by-3-inch) piece dried kombu (about ¼ ounce) (optional)
  • 1 cup soy or oat milk at room temp
  • ¼ cup chinese or japanese sesame paste (or tahini)
  • 1/8 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • ½ tbsp granulated sugar
  • 2 tbsp chili oil, plus more for serving
  • ½ cup frozen corn, defrosted & drained
  • 2 scallions, finely chopped
  • ½ tbsp roasted sesame seeds

instructions

  1. bring a pot of salted water to a boil over high heat. add noodles & cook noodles until al dente, according to package instructions. drain & run under cold water until the noodles are completely cold. set aside to drain
  2. heat a large skillet over medium-high. when hot, add 1 tbsp olive oil & arrange the tofu slices in a single layer. generously season the tofu with about ½ tsp salt & about 1/8 tsp pepper
  3. reduce heat to medium & cook tofu for 2-4 mins until golden. flip the tofu & cook until golden on the other side, about 2-3 mins. remove from the pan & set aside on a plate
  4. to the same pan, add 1 tbsp olive oil & the mushrooms. season with salt & pepper. pan fry until mushrooms are tender & slightly golden, about 6 mins
  5. prepare the broth: pour the vegetable stock into a large pot & add the kombu
  6. bring to a gentle simmer & cook over medium heat, about 7 mins. remove kombu (keep for another use) & turn off the heat
  7. allow to sit for 2 mins, then gradually whisk in milk, adding a little at a time, so it doesn’t curdle. once milk has been added, heat broth over medium until it simmers
  8. in a medium bowl, combine the sesame paste, soy sauce, sesame oil, rice vinegar, sugar & chili oil. divide mixture across four deep noodle bowls
  9. pour the hot broth over the sesame soup base, dividing it evenly among the bowls. whisk to combine the base with the broth
  10. divide the noodles across the bowls & top each bowl with a few slices of tofu, mushrooms, corn, scallion, sesame seeds & extra chili oil
