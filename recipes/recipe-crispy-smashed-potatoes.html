crispy smashed potatoes

serving size: serves 4

recipe origin: recipetin eats tiktok

tags: vegan snack appetizer

ingredients

  • 12-14 small potatoes (or 6-8 medium potatoes)
  • 2 tbsp unsalted (vegan) butter
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • ¾ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp black pepper
  • garlic powder (to taste)
  • onion powder (to taste)
  • finely chopped parsley
  • grated parmesan cheese

instructions

  1. cook potatoes – in boiling, add 1 tbsp to water. alternatively, steam them (about 20 mins)
  2. preheat oven to390˚F
  3. drain the potatoes & let them dry for 5 mins or so
  4. place them on the tray then use a large form or potato masher to squish them, keeping them in one piece (thin = crispier. thick = fluffier insides. more nubbly surface = better crunch!)
  5. leave them on the tray to steam dry for about 5 mins or so – this makes them crispier when baked
  6. bake for 45 mins (small potatoes) to 55 mins (medium potato) or until they are deep golden & crispy. do not flip!
  7. serve hot sprinkled with parsley & parmesan cheese!

navya's thoughts

a perfect snack for when you're craving a crispy/fried potato nibble (slightly elevated from mcdonald's fries, haha!)
